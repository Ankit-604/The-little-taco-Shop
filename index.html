Tacos Trivia

When did tacos did appear in the United States?

The Taco was first introduced to the United States in 1905 . Mexican migrants were coming in to work on railroads and other jobs and started to bring their delicious food with them. Tacos were essentially a street food at this time since they were highly portable and cheap. (Source: Twisted Tacos )
